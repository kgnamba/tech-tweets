Write down the principle you want to explain.

It can be useful to write a question and then answer it. For example, 'What is a turing machine? A turing machine is...'. Write several sentences!


Write down three situations or concrete examples related to your principle.

For example, 'determining whether an algorithm can be solved' or 'the popularity of candy crush'.


Refine these situations: can you make them more specific? can you make them important to the reader?

A reader might not know what 'GPT-2' is. Instead, talk about online bots that automatically generate text.
